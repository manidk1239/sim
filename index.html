The tracker is designed to be used with the browser in fullscreen mode (f11). It can be used without but it may lead to a need to scroll down.

Input the first three letters of the common name for items/songs next to checks. Exceptions are lul, sot, sos for zelda's lullaby, song of time, and song of storms.
Progressive items use str, hoo, wal, and sca.
Start with a capital letter to input hinted items. End with a capital letter to input peeked items. Click an item's icon when the item has been obtained.
Left click check text if the item found is irrelevant to check accessibility. Use middle clicks(or alt + left click) and right clicks for boss keys and small keys, respectively.
Left click on the song check text, or the summary text on the right to toggle if you've found a hint for that check.

Input stones and forest+fire+water medallion into the two rightmost boxes at the bottom of the screen. Use the first two letters of each dungeon or fr for free. example: frshde = free, shadow, deku.
Alternatively, input light, forest, fire, water, shadow and spirit medallions into the rightmost box.
The two boxes to the left of these are used to input spawns relevant to check accessibility for child and adult, respectively (or even irrelevant spawns to help you remember).
Input abbreviations for hint areas in the Barren and WOTH boxes to track those. These are kind of arbitrary but you'll figure it out as you go :)

An alternate way of inputting hints is by putting a location code and the three letter item code (or x for nothing) into the white text area on their own line.
Codes for locations can be found by scrolling down and pressing the "Location Codes" button.

Timer can be started and paused using the Pause key


Location Color Codes:
bright green = can access logically with items you currently have
faded green = can access logically with items you currently have access to
faded yellow = can check out of logic (based on items you currently have access to)
white = known but currently unobtainable


Path Hints(USE S5 PRESET):
bottom woth boxes recognize starting letters of boss names (at least two letters)

can left click items listed under woth locations to the left of the arrow to highlight them yellow or right click to highlight them red
intent: yellow highlight means confirmed poth, red highlight means confirmed not poth.
can click arrow under woth locations to add an item to the right of it by clicking an item in the check summary. these items can be highlighted yellow with left click, surrounded by a red box with middle click and removed with right click.

intent: yellow highlight means confirmed poth, red box means confirmed locked by item to left of arrow, removed means confirmed not locked by item to left of arrow.


Check Summary:
left clicking checked locations will surround it with asterisks (intent: mark as hinted/unhinted). right clicking locations will toggle the item from it as obtained/unobtained.


Animal Companionship:
Gain 1 companion XP with current animal after inputting 20 items into check input boxes or through the hint box.
companion level is equal to 2.5*sqrt of companion XP-1, rounded down.
the tracker will roll a d3 once for every companion level, then reroll animals other than your highest level animal for every result of 1. if it fails to roll that animal, it will repeat the process for your next highest level animal.
in other words, increase rolling odds for a particular animal after 1,2,3,4,6,8,11,13,16,20... seeds played with that animal.


Simulator:
To start the simulator click the "Play the Sim" button and select a spoiler log (only S6 and S5 tested).
The log will appear which keeps track of what you find. It will initially tell you your starting dungeon reward, song, starting age, and spawn.
Click the "Check Pedestal" button to mark medallions and stones.
Click the "Check Adult Spawn" or "Check Child Spawn" button to reveal the spawn for the age you didn't start as.
Left click a check to get it. Right click a check to peek it. You can then left or right click the peeked check again to obtain it.
Click a Gossip Stone check (colored pink) to get a hint. WotH, Path, Barren, Always, and Sometimes hints can then be manually entered in the tracker.
